Welcome

This is a beta version of the Ecoinformatics lab wesite, subject to review, changes and additions. Thanks for understanding the work in progress. For comments and suggestions please email mariona.roige@lincolnuni.ac.nz

The Ecological informatics lab is an open, voluntary collaboration of researchers led by Assoc. Prof. Susan P. Worner at Bio-Protection Research Centre, New Zealand. As a group, we aim to produce new skills, software codes, publications, and services that involve the ecoclimatic assessment of the distribution and spread of invasive species, particularly insects. We investigate prediction and forecasting methods from the disciplines of ecological informatics, computational intelligence, and community ecology. We bring critical analytical techniques to the the study of invasion ecology and biosecurity risk management; areas characterized by complexity and uncertainty.
